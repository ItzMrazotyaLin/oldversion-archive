# Torrent Download Guide

## Recommended Clients
- **qBittorrent** (Free, no ads)
- **Transmission** (Lightweight)

## How to Download
1. Download the `.torrent` file
2. Open it in your torrent client
3. Wait for peers to connect
4. (optional) Verify checksum after download
5. (optional) Start distributing the file

## How to Help Seed
- Leave client running after download completes
- Share ratio of 2.0 or higher is appreciated!
